Coaching Scenario – Prepare

You have studied the GROW model — now it’s time to test yourself in practice. Step into the role of the coach and lead a real-time conversation with a Medical Representative (played by the avatar).

How to work with this scenario

  1. Read Coach brief and Coachee brief above on the SharePoint page.
  2. Click Start Role Play to launch the avatar (Medical Representative).
  3. Lead the conversation using GROW:
    • G — Goal: define the rep’s objective in visit preparation.
    • R — Reality: explore current approach, tools and blockers.
    • O — Options: generate possible improvements together.
    • W — Will: agree on a concrete action plan (steps, timing, check).
  4. Keep the structure; the avatar may gently guide you back to GROW if needed.
  5. Use active listening: follow-ups, summaries, clarifications.
Reflection (2–3 min after the session)
  • What worked well in your questioning?
  • Where did you drift from GROW? How to tighten it next time?
  • What will you change in your next coaching conversation?

Quick tips for impact

  • Ask one powerful question at a time; leave space for thinking.
  • Paraphrase to confirm understanding; avoid advice before O.
  • End with a clear commitment: who, what, by when, how measured.
Sample openers
  • “What would success look like in your preparation over the next 8 weeks?”
  • “Walk me through how you currently plan visits — step by step.”
  • “What options feel realistic for you to try next week?”
